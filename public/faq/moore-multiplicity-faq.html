Moore Multiplicity Model ©

A holistic framework developed by Margaret Moore integrating nine perspectives of mind, motivation, and leadership.

Overview

The Moore Multiplicity Model © integrates decades of personality, motivation, and leadership science into nine distinct yet interconnected dimensions. Each represents a way of perceiving, valuing, and acting in the world.

It’s called “Multiplicity” because each person embodies multiple perspectives — logical, emotional, creative, disciplined, and so on. The model organizes this richness so we can understand and balance the voices within us.

The Nine Dimensions

Everyone has access to all nine dimensions. They are not personality types but perspectives that can be developed to create inner balance and stronger leadership.

1. Autonomy

Authentic / “I care”

Integrity, values, doing what feels right regardless of consequence.

2. Relational

Relational / “I help”

Empathy, compassion, connection, and awareness of others’ needs.

3. Regulator

Servant / “I serve”

Stability, responsibility, discipline, and protection from chaos.

4. Adventurer

Agile / “I flex”

Curiosity, spontaneity, energy, and resilience in uncertainty.

5. Thinker

Conscious / “I see clearly”

Logic, systems thinking, evidence-based analysis, mindfulness.

6. Achiever

Shared / “I share”

Drive, accountability, results orientation, teamwork.

7. Meaning-Maker

Compassion / “I resonate”

Purpose, perspective, wisdom, connection to something larger.

8. Creative

Transformation / “I transform”

Innovation, imagination, synthesis, seeing new possibilities.

9. Confidence

Positive / “I strengthen”

Agency, courage, optimism, belief in one’s own capacity to act.

Which Method Should I Use?

Choose your method based on your available time, complexity of the situation, and depth of analysis needed:

Beginner Methods (Quick & Accessible)

  • QUICK — Fast insights for daily decisions (200-400 words, 2-5 min)
  • CONFLICT — Resolve gridlock between two approaches
  • STAKEHOLDER — Understand multiple people's perspectives
  • NOTES SUMMARY — Organize documents through relevant perspectives

Intermediate Methods (More Thorough)

  • PATTERN — Identify root causes of recurring problems
  • SCENARIO TEST — Compare 2-3 options thoroughly
  • TIME HORIZON — Balance immediate vs. long-term impact

Advanced Methods (Comprehensive)

  • FULL — Complete 9-perspective analysis (800-1200 words)
  • SYNTHESIS — Integrate multiple analyses or levels
  • SYNTHESIS (ALL) — Deep integration across all levels
  • INNER PEACE SYNTHESIS — Resolve internal conflicts
  • COACHING PLAN — Transform analysis into action steps
  • SKILLS — Develop perspective application skills
  • HUMAN HARM CHECK — Comprehensive risk assessment
Tip: If you're unsure, start with QUICK or CONFLICT. The AI will auto-detect which perspectives are most relevant to your situation.

Privacy & Data Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

Data Storage & Retention

  • Encrypted storage — All conversations stored in AWS DynamoDB with encryption at rest
  • 30-day auto-delete — Conversation history automatically expires after 30 days
  • User isolation — Your data is separated by unique user ID; no cross-user access
  • Manual deletion — Delete any conversation at any time via the Delete button

Authentication & Access

  • AWS Cognito — Industry-standard authentication with OAuth 2.0
  • Secure tokens — JWT tokens with expiration for session management
  • No password storage — Authentication handled entirely by AWS
  • HTTPS only — All communication encrypted in transit

AI Processing

  • Anthropic Claude — Powered by Claude API with enterprise security
  • No AI training — Your conversations are NOT used to train AI models
  • Temporary processing — Data sent to Claude only during active analysis
  • Context isolation — Each session is independent; no cross-session learning

Data Sharing

  • No third-party sales — We never sell or share your conversation data
  • Export control — Only you can export via the Share button
  • Admin access — Limited to aggregate usage metrics only (cost tracking, method usage)
  • HIPAA consideration — While secure, this is not a HIPAA-compliant tool; avoid PHI/PII
Best Practice: Avoid entering personally identifiable information (names, addresses, SSN, medical record numbers) or protected health information. Use general descriptions instead.
